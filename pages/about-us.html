About Incozi

We believe every entrepreneur deserves access to world-class business formation and compliance services. Our mission is to make US company formation simple, affordable, and accessible to anyone, anywhere.

Our Mission

To empower global entrepreneurs and businesses by providing accessible, affordable, and reliable US company formation and compliance services. We aim to remove barriers to business ownership and help our clients succeed internationally.

Our Vision

To be the leading trusted partner for US company formation, enabling businesses worldwide to establish and grow their presence in the United States with confidence and ease.
