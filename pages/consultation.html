Two types of consultation — depending on your needs

Choose the right expert to guide your US expansion journey.

Incorporation guidance

Our formation experts guide you through setting up any US entity — whether an LLC, C Corp, or S Corp. We also handle tax and compliance basics for foreign-owned Single-Member LLCs (Form 5472), ensuring you start on solid ground.

Tax strategy session

For complex structures like Multi-Member LLCs, C Corporations, and S Corporations, you need specialized advice. Our experienced tax accountants handle advanced filing requirements and strategic planning to optimize your tax position.

What happens during the consultation?

We dive deep into your business model to pinpoint your exact tax and reporting obligations. Instead of relying on a generic questionnaire that might miss the nuances of your situation, we prefer a direct conversation.

This allows us to clarify complex details and uncover important tax implications you might not be aware of — ensuring nothing is overlooked. (The complexity of US tax law is our job, not yours.)

By the end of the call, you'll have specific answers tailored to your business.

What happens after the consultation?

You'll walk away with a clear roadmap. We'll identify if a different entity structure could save you money on taxes or offer better protection.

You will know exactly which forms need to be filed and how Incozi handles the entire process for you.
